Shadesmar

Shadesmar is a plane of existence. It is the Cognitive Realm, and binds the Physical and Spiritual Realm together.

Shadesmar is an inverse reflection of the Physical Realm, with "water" mirroring land, and land being the reflection of oceans, rivers, and lakes.

These bodies of water are made up of small beads that represent the cognitive aspects of physical world objects, while the land is created out of obsidian.
